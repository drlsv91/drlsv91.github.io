Our Projects

Our main focus has always been the Wicker Park, this park is not
only located in Buffalo, but most of our team members has a strong connection with this park. Most
of us have lived in Buffalo all our lives, and we feel very strongly about preserving out city's environment.

Wicker Park - Protecting the Wild Life

Because the Wicker Park is Located in the middle of the city, there aren't that many species. However, it's very important that we protect the few that still inhabit among us. We work very hard on feeding and providing shelter to different bird and duck species.
